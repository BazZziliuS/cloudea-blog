# Welcome!

Welcome to Cloudea Blog! This is my personal blog where I'll be sharing notes on web development, DevOps, automation, and everything that excites me.

## Why this blog

I wanted a place to write down thoughts, solutions, and tutorials in a structured way — so nothing useful gets lost, and I can share with the community.

## What I'll write about

- **Web development** — Next.js, React, TypeScript, CSS
- **DevOps & automation** — CI/CD, Docker, scripts
- **Tools** — reviews and setups of useful tools
- **Notes** — solutions to interesting problems and findings
